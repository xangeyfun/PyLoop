How to Play

Click to Code

Start your journey by clicking to generate lines of code. Each click adds to your growing codebase and earns you points.

Purchase Upgrades

Use your hard-earned code to buy upgrades. From keyboard shortcuts to AI assistants, there are many ways to boost your productivity.

Automate Your Progress

Let your automated scripts do the work while you're away. Watch your code empire grow even when you're not playing.
